About ITG Manager

What is ITG Manager?

ITG Manager is a tool designed to make it easier to manage your NotITG music libraries.

Features

  • Browse your song packs and tracks
  • Preview songs with built-in audio player
  • View song details including BPM, artist, and difficulty ratings
  • Import content from ZIP files or folders

How to Use

ITGManager automatically scans your StepMania Songs directory. You can browse through your song packs, preview tracks, and import new content directly from the main interface. To configure your Songs directory, visit the Settings page.
